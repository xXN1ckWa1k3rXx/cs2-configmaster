Video settings

FidelityFX Super Resolution (FSR) is a technology similar to NVIDIA's DLSS, which uses upscaling to render frames at a lower resolution and then enlarge them to make the game appear to run at native resolution.
Using FSR may cause distant scenes to appear blurry, which could slightly diminish your gaming experience. Since Counter-Strike 2 uses FSR 1.0, an older version of the technology, it might not significantly improve your performance.
